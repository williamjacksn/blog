>  
> Texas and &ldquo;subtle&rdquo;:  
> Incompatible.
> 
> All of our Summers  
> Burn out abruptly;
> 
> Even our Autumns  
> Feel the need to fall;
> 
> Every Winter  
> Bursts from the freezer;
> 
> And when comes the Spring:  
> Beauty without peer.
>
