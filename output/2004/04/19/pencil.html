Hereʼs to the Pencil

¶ 2004-04-19 07:59:55 +0000

Hereʼs to the Pencil, so noble and thin; Ready for any old state that youʼre in.

Hereʼs to the Pencil; heʼs yellow, yet brave, Taking no thought but your thoughts for to save.

Hereʼs to the Pencil, the one that I hold; With him in my fingers Iʼve strength to be bold.

Hereʼs to the Pencil, the writer of dreams. A new world is born with each stroke, so it seems.

Hereʼs to the Pencil, the number two kind; Heʼll never argue with whatʼs on your mind.
