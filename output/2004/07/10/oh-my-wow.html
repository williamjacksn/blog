Oh My Wow

¶ 2004-07-10 19:58:00 +0000

If you live anywhere near the Austin area, you absolutely must go see The Secret Garden at the Zilker Park outdoor theatre. It plays every Thursday, Friday and Saturday night from now until 7 August. It is free, and it is amazing. The acting and singing are of an unbelievable quality. The show stars two children, and another actor graduated from high school this past spring. These are a talented bunch.
