Say Anything Besides Shoplifting

¶ 2004-10-13 18:49:00 +0000

I still love rain. This batch came out of nowhere and hit the city hard. Cars were sliding everywhere, even into other cars. I got wet; but I got to wear my cabby hat and my newly-dubbed “shoplifting coat”.

No, I do not shoplift, but I think I look cool in my coat. I do not think I look cool because I look like I might engage in illegal activities. I think I look cool because I think I look like John Cusack as Lloyd Dobler in Say Anything. He is cool.

Happy birthday, Aaron!
