More Remarks Concerning Texas and Weather

¶ 2005-10-26 16:53:00 +0000

Texas and “subtle”:
Incompatible.

All of our Summers
Burn out abruptly;

Even our Autumns
Feel the need to fall;

Every Winter
Bursts from the freezer;

And when comes the Spring:
Beauty without peer.
