Spring Break 2006: Day 1

¶ 2006-03-13 17:36:00 +0000

The party has begun. Are you ready for the best spring break ever? I hope so, because it is already upon us.

As all good spring breaks do, this yearʼs spring break started with some yard work. I assisted my mother and two youngest brothers this morning. I found an excuse to dig a hole in our front yard; thatʼs the point of yard work, right? Itʼs an excuse to dig holes. We all took turns with the shovel.

There was also some trimming and raking and sweeping, but you understand none of this is as exciting as digging, for reasons unbeknownst to even myself.

After all that semi-hard work, we had a picnic lunch – in the food court of a local mall. Hey, it was cold outside. After lunch we went to a park and tried to fly a kite and tried to play tennis.
