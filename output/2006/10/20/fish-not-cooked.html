This Fish Is Not Cooked!

¶ 2006-10-20 21:48:00 +0000

Tonight I had for-real sushi. Not fake crabmeat sushi from the student union, the real deal. It was fish and it was not cooked! And it was delicious!

I actually only ate a bit of fish. Most of my dish was eel, which, I am told, needs to be cooked.

So, if Iʼm sick tomorrow I will know why.

My friend Jerry and I have developed a tradition for ourselves here in Austin. Every Friday (almost) we go to a new restaurant for dinner and have an adventure! After dinner we always go to Amyʼs Ice Cream.

Tonightʼs adventure was Sushi Saké. The food is better than the website.
