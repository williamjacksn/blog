Parable of the Good Samaritan

¶ 2012-04-02 19:53:43 +0000

We watched this video as a family tonight. In one scene, the good Samaritan carries the hurt man in his arms. As I carried Emily the same way upstairs for her bath, she said, “Iʼm like the hurt man. Are you the good Samaritan?” “Yes, I am carrying you like the good Samaritan,” I replied. Emily thought for a second, then asked, “Am I the bad Samaritan?”
