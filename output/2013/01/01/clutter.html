Clutter

¶ 2013-01-01 22:01:16 +0000

One of my goals for 2013 is to get rid of clutter. I started by throwing away a bunch of hangers that had been hiding in the back of my daughterʼs closet for at least a year. If I havenʼt used them for a year, chances are I wonʼt use them at all.

I also threw out some Xbox controllers I had in my office. I donʼt even have an Xbox. I havenʼt had one for a long time. I just have this tendency to hold on to things I believe might be useful someday. I can usually make a case for anything being useful eventually.
